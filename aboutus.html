Who are Freya?

I, Freya, love to take pictures of the beauty that life has to offer. For years I only took photography as a hobby, but then making it a name made me me.

When did start taking photograf?

I started taking pictures when my father gifted me a small camera at a very young age. I was taking pictures of everything I saw and they are still in my archive as incredible memories.

Our favorite thing is to sit with my family and talk for hours about those moments.I worked in other jobs for years without thinking about whether I loved it or not, and one day I decided to just do what I love because moments are lived and forgotten, whereas looking back at my childhood memories that I have collected in my own archive is my favorite thing. I said that people should need it and I started to introduce myself professionally and get jobs. I love this job.
